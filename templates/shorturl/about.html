About Shortka

SHORTKA.COM - is a free URL shortener to reduce long links, to hide referrals or forbidden domains in one click.

It's easy like "one, two, three": put your link, press the "Short me!" button and copy your new short nice link for use on social media, email marketing and in other ways what you need.
